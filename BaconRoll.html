In Bacon roll you are a pig escaping from a farm. But this isn’t any old farm, this farm is owned by the big french wolf chef, and he’s not happy you are escaping.

Throughout your escape you will encounter various environments, from dirt, snow to lava and even maybe a little trip to other worlds. Who knows. Maybe you’re an astronaut pig. Maybe you’re a super pig that can fly.

You will have to avoid the evil chefs traps, and roll your way through all kinds of obstacles. Not to mention the amount of food you must NOT eat. But hey, you’re a pig. Can you resist that temptation?

Jump, smash, eat and dodge. That’s the way the pig goes. And all this to rescue your pig princess with the help of your pig fairy-godfather and your brother.
